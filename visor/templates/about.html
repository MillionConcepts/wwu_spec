About the WWU VISOR

The Western Washington University Visible-Infrared Spectral Browser (VISOR) is an online tool for searching, graphing, and downloading publicly-available laboratory spectra. We have developed this tool to aid in our research group's analyses of hyper- and multi-spectral imaging data of planetary surfaces, and to share laboratory data acquired at WWU. We hope that this web tool will be a useful resource for other planetary researchers and the larger scientific community.

We want to hear from you if you have spectra to contribute or if you have ideas about how we can make the site more useful.

The VISOR software is distributed under the permissive BSD 3-Clause license. You can find it on GitHub.

Want to contribute spectra? Contact melissa.rice@wwu.edu.

You can send bug reports by emailing mstclair@millionconcepts.com or filing GitHub issues.
